Whyolet Text:
  • Mobile tasks/text editor
  • with hashtags and gestures.
Hashtags:
  • Click any word.
  • Click tag button.
  • Result: #hashtag is opened.
Gestures:
  • Swipe Left: deLete.
  • Swipe Right: tomorRow.
  • send click: tomorrow.
  • send long-click: select a date.
  • arrow_forward click: replace.
  • arrow_forward long-click: replace all.
  • Another button long-click: repeat.
  • Pinch: zoom.
Standard gestures:
  • 3 clicks: select line.
  • 2 clicks or long-click: select word(s),
  • 1 more click: select one word.
  • Empty space long-click: select all.
Advanced:
  • Open #path/file.html
  • Type or copy-paste:
    <script>if (confirm("?")) alert("!")</script>
  • Click menu east file.html
  • Open the downloaded file.
Privacy:
  • Your data is kept locally on your device
  • unless you sync it to your Google Drive
  • or download and upload it somewhere
  • or copy and share it yourself.
